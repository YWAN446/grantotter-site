Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GrantOtter is operated by Yuke Wang, Vernon Hills, IL. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address and encrypted password, collected when you create an account.
  • Researcher profiles — AI-generated profiles based on your name, institution, and publicly available sources. If you upload a CV, it is processed server-side to extract text and is not shared externally.
  • Application data — grant applications, draft concepts, biosketches, checklists, and documents you create or upload within the app.
  • Usage analytics — anonymized usage events (e.g., tab visits, features used) collected via Google Analytics to help improve the product. No personal identifiers are linked to these events.

How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the GrantOtter service.
  • To generate researcher profiles, match grants, and produce biosketches using AI.
  • To send grant match results to your email when you request it.
  • To improve the product based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

Third-party services

GrantOtter uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database and file storage. Your profiles, applications, and documents are stored in a private Supabase project with row-level security (RLS). Only you can access your own data.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing for profile generation, grant matching, brainstorming, and biosketch generation. Your data is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's data processing agreement applies.
  • Voyage AI — semantic search embeddings for the collaborator search feature. Researcher names and profile text from the shared institution pool are sent to Voyage AI for embedding generation.
  • Google Analytics — anonymized website and app usage tracking.
  • Gmail (SMTP) — used to send grant match results to your email address when you request it.

Your data is never used for AI training

Your profiles, applications, biosketches, and documents are never used to train AI models — by GrantOtter or any third party. Your content is used solely to generate results for you within your session.

Data security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest via Supabase.
  • Row-level security (RLS) ensures each user can only access their own data — no other user can read yours.
  • Passwords are fully encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • Uploaded documents are stored in a private, access-controlled Supabase Storage bucket.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing grantotter42@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Cookies

GrantOtter uses cookies for authentication session management (via Supabase Auth) and for Google Analytics. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of GrantOtter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email grantotter42@gmail.com.
